Projects

Uncovering U.S. Macroeconomic Dynamics: A VAR-Based Analysis of Causality and Economic Shocks

This project analyzes long-term interactions among key U.S. macroeconomic indicators – Personal Consumption Expenditure (PCE), Money Supply (M2), Industrial Production (INDPRO), and Consumer Sentiment (UMCSENT) – using Vector Autoregression (VAR). Through Granger causality, Impulse Response Functions (IRF), and Forecast Error Variance Decomposition (FEVD), the study explores how shocks in one variable propagate to others, revealing demand-driven dynamics and feedback loops between consumption and production. Key insights show that M2 responds to real-side variables, PCE and INDPRO reinforce each other, while UMCSENT evolves largely autonomously. These findings provide valuable inputs for policy design, financial risk management, and business strategy, emphasizing the importance of real-time data monitoring for economic decision-making.

Customer Segmentation & Churn Prediction using RFM Analysis and Machine Learning

Developed a data-driven analytics solution for a UK-based online retail dataset, combining RFM analysis with K-Means clustering to segment customers into high-value, loyal, and at-risk groups. Conducted in-depth EDA to uncover purchasing patterns and revenue drivers, followed by feature engineering on behavioral attributes such as visit frequency, product diversity, and basket size over the last four months. Performance Analytics Dashboard for Retail Sales

Designed and developed a Power BI dashboard to provide actionable insights into sales performance, customer satisfaction, and outlet-level efficiency for a retail business. The dashboard integrates structured data modeling and KPI-driven analytics, featuring dynamic metrics such as average rating, number of items sold, and total sales. Interactive slicers allow filtering by outlet type, size, and location, enabling tailored views for different business needs. Visualizations include line graphs, pie charts, tree maps, clustered bar charts, and scatter plots, delivering a clear narrative of operational performance. The solution empowers data-driven decision-making in sales strategy, inventory distribution, and outlet optimization, improving revenue forecasting and customer engagement.
